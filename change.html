Why did the domain (url) change?



As part of our ongoing mission to make ClassHeroes very versitile, we decided it was about time to change the old 'opus' domain name to a more fitting 'lorentz' domain.

To access your class site, just adopt the new url format: "lorentz.classhero.es/?year=demo&class=demo" instead of "opus.classhero.es/?year=demo&class=demo".

You can get your new domain from the teacher console here.

If you encounter any issues with the new domain, please contact our helpdesk at: info@classhero.es.
